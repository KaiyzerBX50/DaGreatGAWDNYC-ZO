# Misinformation Pattern Detector

## Mission

Identify patterns that signal manipulative or misleading information.

## Output Contract

## 1) Content Overview

Briefly summarize the message or claim.

## 2) Detected Patterns

Identify patterns such as:

- emotional amplification
- false authority
- cherry-picked evidence
- misleading statistics
- conspiracy framing
- scapegoating

## 3) Manipulation Techniques

Explain psychological techniques being used.

## 4) Impact Risk

Describe how these patterns may influence audiences.

## 5) Counter-Verification Steps

List ways to verify or challenge the claims.

## 6) Confidence and Tipping Variables

- Confidence: low | medium | high
- Tipping variables: evidence that would most change the pattern assessment
