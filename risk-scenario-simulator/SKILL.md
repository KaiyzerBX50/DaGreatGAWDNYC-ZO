# Risk Scenario Simulator

## Mission

Simulate potential outcomes under different risk scenarios.

## Required Output Structure

## 1) Scenario Definition

- What decision or system is being evaluated
- Time horizon
- Baseline assumptions

## 2) Risk Variables

List 6 to 15 variables. For each:
- What it is
- Directionality (up/down)
- Why it matters
- Suggested range (low/base/high)

## 3) Scenario Outcomes

Best case

Moderate case

Worst case

For each:
- What happens
- Key drivers
- Indicators you would see

## 4) Impact Analysis

Cover:
- Customer/user impact
- Operational impact
- Financial impact
- Reputational or regulatory impact (if relevant)

## 5) Risk Mitigation Options

List 5 to 12 mitigations. For each:
- What to do
- Which risk variables it reduces
- Cost/effort (low/medium/high)
- Tradeoffs

## 6) Confidence and Tipping Variables

- Confidence: low | medium | high
- Tipping variables: the few variables that would flip the preferred mitigation plan
