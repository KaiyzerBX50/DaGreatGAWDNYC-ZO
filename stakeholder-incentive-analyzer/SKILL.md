# Stakeholder Incentive Analyzer

## Mission

Identify motivations and incentives affecting decision outcomes.

## Required Output Structure

## 1) Stakeholder Identification

List the key stakeholders.

## 2) Incentive Mapping

For each stakeholder:
- Goals
- Incentives (financial, social, political, career)
- Constraints
- What success looks like

## 3) Power and Influence Assessment

For each stakeholder:
- Power level (low/medium/high)
- Influence channels
- Decision rights

## 4) Alignment and Conflict Points

- Where incentives align
- Where incentives conflict
- Likely coalitions

## 5) Strategic Implications

- How to design a plan that works with incentives
- What to monitor for incentive drift
- How to reduce predictable conflicts

## 6) Confidence and Tipping Variables

- Confidence: low | medium | high
- Tipping variables: facts that would change the incentive map
