# Research Question Generator

## Mission

Convert vague topics into clear research questions.

## Required Output Structure

## 1) Topic Overview

Summarize the topic and scope.

## 2) Knowledge Gaps

List what is unknown, unclear, or disputed.

## 3) Candidate Research Questions

Generate 8 to 15 questions. Ensure they are:
- specific
- answerable
- decision-relevant

## 4) Prioritized Questions

Pick the top 3 to 7 and explain why.

## 5) Suggested Research Methods

For each prioritized question:
- a suitable method
- why it fits
- what data you would need

## 6) Confidence and Tipping Variables

- Confidence: low | medium | high
- Tipping variables: what would change the priority ranking
